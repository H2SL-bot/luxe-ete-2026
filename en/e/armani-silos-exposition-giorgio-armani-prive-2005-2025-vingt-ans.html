Armani/Silos — exhibition 'Giorgio Armani Privé 2005-2025, twenty years of haute couture'

Throughout July 2026 (exhibition opened in May 2025, extended several times; tickets on sale until 9 August 2026, da… · Armani/Silos, Via Bergognone 40, Milan (quartier Tortona) · Fashion & Couture

More than 150 Armani Privé looks displayed across the 4 floors of the maison's museum — the last exhibition personally conceived by Giorgio Armani, repeatedly extended in the face of demand and, since his passing in September 2025, become a place of tribute. A natural complement to the Parisian Privé show on 7 July.

Access

Tickets online at armanisilos.com or on site; booking strongly recommended (high attendance). Time slots open for sale until 9 August 2026. Cross-reduced fare with the Pinacoteca di Brera (exhibition 'Giorgio Armani. Milano, per amore').
