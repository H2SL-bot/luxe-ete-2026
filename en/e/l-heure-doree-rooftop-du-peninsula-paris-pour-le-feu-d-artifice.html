L'Heure Dorée — the Peninsula Paris rooftop for the fireworks

open all summer 2026 (since late May), key evening on 13 July · Rooftop 'L'Heure Dorée' au 6e étage du Peninsula Paris, 19 avenue Kléber, 75116 · Palace Hotels & Fine Dining

The Peninsula's summer terrace with a view of the Eiffel Tower and the Sacré-Cœur. Sharing plates by two-Michelin-starred chef David Bizet, desserts by Anne Coruble, exclusive The Macallan cocktails, Laurent-Perrier champagne, and a Belle Époque playlist by Bon Entendeur. A prime spot for the 13 July fireworks.

Line-up : David Bizet (2 Michelin stars), Anne Coruble, Bon Entendeur

Access

No reservation — capacity limited to 70 people (50 seated, 20 standing): arrive early, or go through the hotel concierge for guests. peninsula.com/paris
