Twiga Beach Club Forte dei Marmi — cena spettacolo

Summer 2026 season (Vesta cena spettacolo dinner-shows and Twiga dance nights) — the season actually opens as early as April 3, with the heart of summer running June to September. · Twiga Beach Club / Vesta Versilia — front de mer · Festivals & Music

The most exclusive beach club in Versilia, born from an idea by Flavio Briatore and now signed by LMDV (Leonardo Maria Del Vecchio) under the name Vesta. By night, a dinner-show with live performances sets the stage for the dancing that follows, driven by the finest Italian and international DJs — the very archetype of the jet-set evening, between umbrella pines and the sea.

Line-up : Exceptional guests and DJs by calendar (themed evenings, live guests) — line-up published as the season unfolds.

How to get in

Twiga Versilia / Vesta Versilia (LMDV Hospitality)

Access by booking a cena-spettacolo (arrival around 21:00–22:00) or a tavolo dopo-cena (after 00:30) via the official site twigafortedeimarmi.com; ticketing only for special events. Many evenings sell out well in advance.
