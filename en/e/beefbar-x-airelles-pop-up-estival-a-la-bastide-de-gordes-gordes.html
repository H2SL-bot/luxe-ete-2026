Beefbar x Airelles — summer pop-up at La Bastide de Gordes

27 June – 28 August 2026, every evening from 7.30pm · Airelles Gordes, La Bastide (palace), Gordes · Palace Hotels & Fine Dining

Summer residency of the Beefbar brand at the Airelles palace hotel: Black Angus, Japanese Wagyu, refined street food and sunset dinners overlooking the Luberon and the Alpilles. The gastronomic event of the summer in Gordes.

Line-up : Beefbar concept (Riccardo Giraudi), the palace's international clientele

Access

Book online via SevenRooms (sevenrooms.com/explore/beefbargordes) or through the hotel; open to non-residents.
