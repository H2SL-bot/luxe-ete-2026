Dolce & Gabbana x Casa Amor — 'DG Resort' takeover (Carretto Siciliano theme)

Summer 2026, until 31 October 2026 (open throughout July) · Casa Amor, plage de Pampelonne, chemin des Moulins, Ramatuelle · Fashion & Couture

For 2026 Dolce & Gabbana renews its 'DG Resort' takeover of the Casa Amor beach club in the colours of Sicily (the Carretto Siciliano motif on cushions, towels, parasols, right through to the golden DG Logo bag), with a dedicated pop-up store ('Blu Mediterraneo' interiors) and Mediterranean cuisine by chef Zouhair alongside international guest chefs. Confirmed by the official Dolce&Gabbana website (DG Resort 2026); the takeover runs until 31 October 2026.

Line-up : Dolce&Gabbana

Access

Reserve a table or bed via casaamor-sttropez.com / Moma Group, or through your hotel concierge. Strong demand in July: book several days ahead.
