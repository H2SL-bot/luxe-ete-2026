Bastille Day Fireworks and Ball in Saint-Tropez

14 July 2026 · Môle Jean-Réveille, Vieux Port de Saint-Tropez (feu tiré d'une barge en mer) · Other

National Day pyrotechnic display over the gulf, framed by the yachts lying at anchor, followed by the public ball on the jetty of the mole. Confirmed for Tuesday 14 July 2026 by the town's official website and several regional listings.

Access

Free. For a premium vantage point: a terrace table on the port, dinner aboard a yacht (charter through local agencies) or a dedicated evening on the Pampelonne beaches — book through concierges (Byblos, Cheval Blanc, Airelles Pan Deï). Town hall information: 04 94 55 90 00.
