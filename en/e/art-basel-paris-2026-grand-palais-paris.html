Art Basel Paris 2026 — Grand Palais

From October 21 to 25, 2026 · Grand Palais (VIP preview 21-22, public 23-25) · Grand Palais · Art & Auctions

The jet-set rendezvous of the Paris autumn: 206 galleries from 41 countries under the nave of the Grand Palais for the 5th edition, the first led by Karim Crippa. The VIP days on October 21-22 gather billionaire collectors, luxury houses and celebrities ahead of the public opening.

Line-up : Major international collectors, leading galleries, partner luxury houses

How to get in

Art Basel (MCH Group) — Paris edition at the Grand Palais

The free route goes through the galleries: a VIP preview invitation is obtained from a participating gallery where you are a client — or a serious prospect. Otherwise, Premium tickets (vernissage on October 22) and public tickets are on sale at artbasel.com. Press accreditation opens the VIP days to journalists who can show a media affiliation.
