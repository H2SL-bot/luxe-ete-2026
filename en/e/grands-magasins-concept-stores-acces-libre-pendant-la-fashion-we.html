Department stores & concept stores — free access during Fashion Week

All through Fashion Week (28 Sept–6 Oct 2026) · Le Bon Marché, Galeries Lafayette, Printemps, Leclaireur (Paris) · Fashion & Couture

The easiest way to experience designer fashion during Fashion Week, entirely free of charge: Paris's department stores and concept stores stage pop-ups, capsule collections and windows spotlighting emerging designers. Le Bon Marché (LVMH) regularly hosts fashion happenings; the concept stores showcase the avant-garde.

Line-up : Pop-up designers, emerging “Made in Paris” labels, the avant-garde

How to get in

Le Bon Marché Rive Gauche (LVMH), Galeries Lafayette, Printemps, Leclaireur

FREE, complimentary entry in store, no reservation required: discover designer pop-ups, Fashion Week capsule collections and emerging labels (e.g. the “Made in Paris” selection at Galeries Lafayette Champs-Élysées). Le Bon Marché (LVMH) regularly stages fashion and designer happenings on the Rive Gauche. For client soirées and preview events (by invitation), reach out to the house's client relations team or your personal advisor. The zero-euro way to experience Fashion Week from the inside.
