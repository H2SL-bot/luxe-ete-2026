Grandes Eaux Nocturnes at Versailles — July editions (including the special Bastille Day evening on 14 July)

Saturdays 4 ('American Independence' edition), 11, 18, 25 July + Tuesday 14 July 2026, the only non-Saturday edition of the… · Jardins du château de Versailles · Art & Auctions

A night-time stroll through Le Nôtre's gardens: illuminated fountains, light effects, pyrotechnics and a grand fireworks display. Special Bastille Day edition on Tuesday 14 July 2026 with an expanded baroque programme and larger-scale fireworks. Optional before the show: the Sérénade Royale in the Hall of Mirrors (a performance-tour with dancers, musicians and fencers in baroque costume).

Line-up : Groupe F (pyrotechnics)

Access

Tickets at chateauversailles-spectacles.fr or +33 1 30 83 78 00; combined Sérénade Royale + Grandes Eaux Nocturnes ticket approx. EUR 63; also on sale at versailles-tourisme.com. Book online in advance, as the July Saturdays and 14 July sell out.
