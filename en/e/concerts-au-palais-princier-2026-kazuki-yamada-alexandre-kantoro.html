Concerts au Palais Princier 2026 — Kazuki Yamada & Alexandre Kantorow (OPMC, season closing)

6 August 2026, 9.30pm · Cour d'Honneur du Palais Princier de Monaco · Art & Auctions

Closing concert of the 2026 season of the Concerts au Palais Princier and Kazuki Yamada's final concert as artistic and musical director of the OPMC before his departure from Monaco. A Liszt (Piano Concerto No. 2) / Ravel programme with Alexandre Kantorow, winner of the 2019 Tchaikovsky Competition. Listed SOLD OUT (waiting list) on the official website from July 2026.

Line-up : Kazuki Yamada (conducting, final concert as artistic and musical director of the OPMC), Alexandre Kantorow (piano)

Access

Concert listed COMPLET (SOLD OUT – WAITING LIST) on opmc.mc — join the waiting list via the OPMC box office: Atrium of the Casino de Monte-Carlo, tel. +377 92 00 13 70, or opmc.mc / montecarloticket.com. Face-value prices: 20 € to 175 € (Carré d'or). Formal attire required, jacket and tie obligatory for gentlemen.
