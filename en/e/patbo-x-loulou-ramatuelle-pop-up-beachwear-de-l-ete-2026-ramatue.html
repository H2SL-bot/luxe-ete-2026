PatBO x Loulou Ramatuelle — Summer 2026 beachwear pop-up

Loulou's 2026 season: 23 April - 3 October 2026 (pop-up active in July) · Loulou Ramatuelle, boulevard Patch, plage de Pampelonne, Ramatuelle · Fashion & Couture

The Brazilian label PatBO (embroidery and beachwear, São Paulo/New York) takes over Loulou Ramatuelle for the summer of 2026: pop-up store, a complete takeover (menus, uniforms, cushions, beach cabanas in exclusive prints) and pieces developed especially for Loulou. Confirmed by the Brazilian fashion press and the 2026 pop-up guides.

Access

Table/sunbed reservations at loulou-ramatuelle.com or by telephone; go through the concierge (Airelles, Cheval Blanc, Byblos) for the best tables in July.
