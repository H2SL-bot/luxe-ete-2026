Yerai Cortés in concert in the Labyrinthe Miró (Fondation Maeght)

11 August 2026 · Fondation Maeght, Labyrinthe Miró · Festivals & Music

An exceptional concert by flamenco guitar prodigy Yerai Cortés (Alicante), the first flamenco guitarist nominated for the Latin Grammy for Best New Artist (2024) and winner of the 2025 Goya for Best Documentary for La Guitarra Flamenca de Yerai Cortés — one of the nocturnes of the Fondation's summer 2026 season.

Line-up : Yerai Cortés (Latin Grammy nomination 2024, Goya 2025 for Best Documentary)

Access

Online ticketing via Weezevent at fondation-maeght.com (tickets non-exchangeable and non-refundable)
