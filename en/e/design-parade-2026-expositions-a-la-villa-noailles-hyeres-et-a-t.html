Design Parade 2026 — exhibitions at the villa Noailles (Hyères) and in Toulon (20+10 anniversary edition)

Festival from 25 to 28 June 2026 (before July); exhibitions on view throughout July: until 30 August 2026 at the villa N… · Villa Noailles (Hyères) et Chapelle/Médiathèque Chalucet, quartier de la créativité (Toulon) · Art & Auctions

A double-anniversary edition: 20 years of Design Parade (object design, Hyères) and 10 years of the interior architecture festival (Toulon). The finalists' exhibitions remain on view at the villa Noailles until 30 August — exceptionally, the entire villa hosts the two competitions together. Line-up : Laura Gonzalez, Sofia Lagerkvist and Anna Lindgren (studio Front), David Giroire; the legacy of patrons the Vicomte and Vicomtesse de Noailles

Access

Free admission to the villa Noailles and to the Toulon exhibition venues during opening hours, no reservation required; for the festival's private views and dinners (June), invitation via the villa Noailles, the partner houses or membership of the Amis de la villa Noailles.
