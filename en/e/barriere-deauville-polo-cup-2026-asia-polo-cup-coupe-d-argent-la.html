Barrière Deauville Polo Cup 2026 (Asia Polo Cup, Coupe d'Argent, Ladies Polo Cup, Coupe de Bronze)

6-30 August 2026 (Asia Polo Cup 6-9, Coupe d'Argent 10-16, Ladies Polo Cup 11-14, Coupe de Bronze 17-29 August) · Deauville International Polo Club, hippodrome de Deauville-La Touques (accès boulevard Mauger) · Polo · Motoring · Sailing

A month of polo at its pinnacle: five tournaments, including the legendary cups created by the Barrière family, gathering the finest international teams and patrons. New for 2026: the Asia Polo Cup (6-9 August) opens the season.

Line-up : International patrons and professional players from the European circuit

Access

Access to the stands is traditionally free and open to all. VIP tables, hospitality and evening events through the Deauville Polo Club: contact@deauvillepoloclub.com, +33 7 70 07 92 92, deauvillepoloclub.com.
