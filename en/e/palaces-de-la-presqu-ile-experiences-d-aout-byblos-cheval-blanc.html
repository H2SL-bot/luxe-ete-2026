Palaces of the Peninsula — August Experiences (Byblos, Cheval Blanc, Airelles Château de la Messardière, La Réserve Ramatuelle)

all of August 2026 · Byblos (av. Paul Signac), Cheval Blanc St-Tropez (plage de la Bouillabaisse), Airelles Château de la Messardi… · Palace Hotels & Fine Dining

The peninsula's four palace hotels bring together, throughout August, exceptional dinners, luxury-house pop-ups (ephemeral Dior, Chanel and Vuitton boutiques in town) and private house soirées — the 2026 season marked by the Cédric Grolet x Airelles Messardière partnership and the new Starck décor at La Réserve à la Plage.

Line-up : Arnaud Donckele (3-Michelin-star, La Vague d'Or), Maxime Frédéric, Cédric Grolet (Messardière), Philippe Starck (La Réserve à la Plage) ; international UHNW clientele

How to get in

The 4 houses: Byblos (Groupe Floirat), Cheval Blanc (LVMH), Airelles Collection, La Réserve (Michel Reybier Hosp.)

Open access, no invitation required: book tables, treatments and loungers directly with the concierge desks (contacts opposite). Staying at one of the houses grants priority on August's time slots (Zuma in residence at Byblos, live evenings at Byblos Beach on Fridays in July-August, Byblos spa Day Pass published). Private hire on request at Byblos Beach. A private concierge or an LHW/Virtuoso agency unlocks fully booked slots. No charity table documented.
