Exhibition 'Africa Fashion' — Musée du quai Branly-Jacques Chirac

31 March - 12 July 2026 · Musée du quai Branly-Jacques Chirac, Galerie Jardin, Paris 7e · Art & Auctions

Exhibition conceived by the Victoria and Albert Museum in London (after 6 international stops), celebrating more than sixty years of African fashion design, in dialogue with the museum's collections (Galerie Jardin). Final days during couture week. Currently on view (ending 12 July).

Access

Online ticketing via the museum (quaibranly.fr) or on site.
