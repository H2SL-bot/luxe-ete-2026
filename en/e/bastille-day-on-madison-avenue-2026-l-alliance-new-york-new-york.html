Bastille Day on Madison Avenue 2026 (L'Alliance New York)

Sunday 12 July 2026 · Madison Avenue (59e-63e rues) et 60e rue entre 5e et Park Avenue, Manhattan · Festivals & Music

New York's largest French festival, 2026 theme "Montmartre to Manhattan - 250 years of Franco-American creativity": 60+ French gastronomy and craft stalls, performances, a 1,225 sq ft giant Game of the Goose, a Citroën rally, and The French Garden premium shopping area (Printemps New York, Angelina Paris, La Maison du Chocolat).

Line-up : Organised by L'Alliance New York (formerly FIAF) with the Consulate General of France and the Madison Avenue BID; concert featuring Black M, Laurent Voulzy, Michel Polnareff

Access

Street freely open and free of charge; tasting party at L'Alliance's Skyroom by ticket ($75 non-members, $65 members) via lallianceny.org; evening concert in Central Park free (arrive early).
