Chanel — summer boutique at the villa La Mistralée

Summer 2026 season (traditionally May-October) — open in July, exact 2026 dates not published · Villa La Mistralée, 1 avenue du Général Leclerc, Saint-Tropez · Fashion & Couture

Since 2009, Chanel has taken up its summer quarters at the villa La Mistralée, a pop-up boutique decorated by Peter Marino in the spirit of La Pausa, Gabrielle Chanel's own villa: iconic bags (11.12, 2.55, Chanel 25), eyewear, scarves and costume jewellery. A well-documented recurring boutique, though the 2026 opening dates have not been published online (the official Chanel page is live, with no calendar).

Access

Free access to the boutique; private appointments and rare pieces through a Chanel sales associate or your hotel concierge.
