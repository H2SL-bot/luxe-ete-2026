41st World Diamond Congress (WFDB)

12-15 July 2026 · PARKROYAL COLLECTION Marina Bay · Jewellery & Watchmaking

The World Federation of Diamond Bourses' global congress returns to Singapore after a 38-year absence (the first time since 1988), at the PARKROYAL COLLECTION Marina Bay — the same venue as in 1988. Billed as a "Davos of diamonds", hosted by the Diamond Exchange of Singapore: five panels on the natural diamond value chain, opening ceremony on 13 July.

Line-up : WFDB (president Yoram Dvash), Pritesh Patel (CEO of GIA)

Access

Reserved for professionals/delegates: paid online registration at worlddiamondcongress.com (via the WFDB member diamond bourses); contact worlddiamondcongress@thetriumph.com
