Trade shows — Tranoï, Première Classe, JILL, WOMAN Paris

1–5 October 2026 (varies by show) · professional showrooms · Palais Brongniart & Jardin des Tuileries, Paris · Fashion & Couture

Fashion Week's major trade shows, where buyers and press discover hundreds of brands. Free trade access on registration for most; Première Classe (accessories, Tuileries) also admits visitors with a paid badge.

Line-up : Hundreds of brands and independent designers, international buyers

How to get in

Tranoï (GL Events); WSN — Première Classe, JILL, WOMAN/MAN Paris

Buyers & trade visitors: free e-pass on registration with Tranoï (tranoi.com/e/paris/registration); WSN badge for Première Classe / JILL / WOMAN Paris via register.premiere-classe.com. Press: free accreditation (press@wsn-events.com). Curious members of the public: Première Classe admits a visitor badge at €50 when pre-registered (€60 on site). It is the most affordable paid route to approach the Fashion Week ecosystem.
