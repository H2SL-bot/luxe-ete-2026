Guild Hall Summer Gala 2026

7 August 2026 (Friday) · Guild Hall, 158 Main Street, East Hampton · Art & Auctions

The annual gala of the Guild Hall museum and arts centre, honouring Andrea Grover for her ten years at the helm of the institution, with a preview of the exhibitions Ross Bleckner: Never The Less and Eric Freeman: The Volume of Color.

Line-up : The New York art world, collectors, East End artists

How to get in

Guild Hall (museum and performing arts centre, 158 Main Street, East Hampton, NY). Annual summer gala, 2026 edition honouring Andrea Grover (Executive Director, 10 years at the head of the institution), co-chaired by Barbara Lane and Galia Meiri-Stawski.

Friday 7 August 2026: 6 pm cocktails and preview of the exhibitions (Ross Bleckner: Never The Less; Eric Freeman: The Volume of Color), 7 pm dinner-dance in the gardens, 9:30 pm After Party. Tickets and tables at guildhall.org/events/2026-summer-gala. The gala dinner is sold out (waiting list by email at events@guildhall.org, confirmed on the official page) — more accessible options to mingle with the same crowd: the cocktails ticket (6 pm) and the After Party ticket (9:30 pm), purchasable separately. Tax deductibility indicated (160 $ of the dinner, 75 $ of the cocktail, 50 $ of the after). Sponsor tables and partnerships via Kendra Korczak (631-324-0806 x116).
