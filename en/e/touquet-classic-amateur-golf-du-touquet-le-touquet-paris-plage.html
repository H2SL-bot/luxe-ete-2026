Touquet Classic Amateur – Golf du Touquet

25 – 28 August 2026 (practice day and welcome on Monday 24 August, gala dinner on Wednesday 26 August) · Le Touquet Golf Resort (Resonance Golf Collection), parcours La Mer (links historique Harry Colt) et La Forêt · Polo · Motoring · Sailing

A high-end amateur tournament over 4 rounds across the legendary 'La Mer' links and 'La Forêt': stroke play/stableford by division, daily prizes, 4 buffet lunches and the gala dinner on 26 August included. The chic golfing event of late summer in Le Touquet. Entry list published, waiting list open.

Access

Entry list published, waiting list active (updated as withdrawals occur); contact: 03 21 06 28 00 / letouquet.competition@resonance.golf.
