Ellen Hermanson Foundation Summer Gala 2026

Friday 24 July 2026 · Bridgehampton Tennis and Surf Club (au bord de l'ocean) · Other

Chic charity gala in support of breast cancer care (Ellen Hermanson Breast Center at Stony Brook Southampton Hospital); the 2026 edition honouring Geralyn Lucas, author and advocate ("Why I Wore Lipstick to My Mastectomy"). One of the most sought-after seaside dinners of late July in the Hamptons.

Line-up : Geralyn Lucas (2026 honoree); the classic NYC-Hamptons summer set (finance, fashion, media)

How to get in

The Ellen Hermanson Foundation, organizer of its annual summer gala (30th anniversary edition) in support of breast cancer care (Ellen Hermanson Breast Center, Stony Brook Southampton Hospital).

Paid gala open by ticket (no invitation required): individual tickets from 1 250 $ (750 $ for those under 39), tables and sponsor packages above that, online purchase at ellenhermanson.org/2026summergala. To book a table or a partnership: Mady Schuman (mady.schuman@ellenhermanson.org, 631-324-2802). Friday, July 24, 2026, Bridgehampton Tennis and Surf Club (oceanfront); 30th anniversary edition honoring Geralyn Lucas.
