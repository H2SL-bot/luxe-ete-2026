Rockwool France Sail Grand Prix — SailGP Saint-Tropez 2026

Saturday 12 and Sunday 13 September 2026 · Bay of Saint-Tropez · Baie de Saint-Tropez · Polo · Motoring · Sailing

French stop of the Rolex SailGP championship: 14 nations — a historic expanded format — compete on flying F50 catamarans in the Bay of Saint-Tropez. High-end hospitality overlooking the racecourse, a grand-spectacle atmosphere much favoured by the sailing world and the jet-set of Saint-Tropez's September season.

Line-up : Competitive sailing world, Rolex and Rockwool sponsors, jet-set of Saint-Tropez's September season

How to get in

SailGP (Rolex SailGP championship), Rockwool France Sail Grand Prix stop

Two tiers: public grandstand tickets on sale at sailgp.com, and premium hospitality packages (waterfront lounge, catering) on the same ticketing platform. The spectacle can also be watched for free from the heights and the shoreline of the bay; for the society experience, aim for the official lounge.
