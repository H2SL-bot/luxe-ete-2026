Paris Fashion Week — Ready-to-Wear Spring/Summer 2027

28 September to 6 October 2026 · Paris (various venues) · Divers lieux (Fédération de la Haute Couture et de la Mode) · Fashion & Couture

The highlight of the season: Chanel, Dior, Saint Laurent and Paris high society for the apotheosis of the Big Four. Fashion's global consecration, the absolute epicentre of Parisian high society and the front row.

Line-up : Great Parisian houses, front-row celebrities, global press

How to get in

Fédération de la Haute Couture et de la Mode (FHCM), organiser of the official calendar

Four avenues of access to the official calendar shows (28 Sept–6 Oct 2026): 1) PRESS — free accreditation with the FHCM (accreditation@fhcm.paris), followed by an invitation from the press office of the house in question; 2) listed professional BUYERS; 3) CLIENTS / VIC — cultivating a lasting client relationship with a house (the same advisor, a purchase history) opens the door to invitations and the front row; 4) friends of the house & public figures. FHCM accreditation is necessary but not sufficient: each house decides its own guest list. The detailed show-by-show calendar is published in early September.
