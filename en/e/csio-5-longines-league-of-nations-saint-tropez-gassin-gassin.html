CSIO 5* Longines League of Nations — Saint-Tropez-Gassin

From Wednesday 16 to Sunday 20 September 2026 · Haras de Gassin · Haras de Gassin, Polo Club Saint-Tropez · Polo · Motoring · Sailing

Final worldwide qualifying leg of the Longines League of Nations before the Barcelona final: the world's 10 best show jumping nations (Germany, Ireland, United States, France…) at the Haras de Gassin. The gulf's new ultra-high-end equestrian rendezvous, in the setting of the Polo Club Saint-Tropez.

Line-up : World show jumping elite, equestrian owners and sponsors, gulf society

How to get in

FEI (Longines League of Nations) and Polo Club Saint-Tropez, Haras de Gassin

Event announced as open to the public by the club — daytime access to the competitions is the simple route. For the society tier (tables, VIP terraces at the ringside), go through the Polo Club Saint-Tropez via its official website; detailed ticketing had not yet been published as of 21 July 2026.
