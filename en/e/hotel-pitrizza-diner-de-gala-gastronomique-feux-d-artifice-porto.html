Hotel Pitrizza — Gastronomic Gala Dinner & Fireworks

Ferragosto week — around 14 August (2026 date not yet officially published) · Hotel Pitrizza, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Porto Cervo · Other

TO BE CONFIRMED for 2026: a recurring event (fireworks over the bay, dinner-show during Ferragosto week), but the only detailed source available describes the 2024 edition (costasmeralda.it calendar dated July 2024, echoed by topreservation). Date and line-up for 2026 not officially confirmed — 14 August is the date of previous editions. Included as a palace gala frequented by the jet-set, subject to confirmation.

Line-up : Chef Daniele Turco; Ruinart Champagne; live music

How to get in

Hotel Pitrizza, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Costa Smeralda (Smeralda Holding group, distribution Marriott / The Luxury Collection), setting for the Ferragosto-week gala dinner with fireworks over the bay.

The 2026 edition has not yet been officially published (the dinner is traditionally held around 14-15 August, with the hotel reopening on 29 May 2026): there is no public ticketing. Route of access: book the dinner directly with the hotel's reservations office (+39 0789 930111), given as a priority to the palace's residents and their guests; going through the hotel's concierge — or that of another palace hotel on the Costa Smeralda — improves your chances. Confirm the date, programme and price (very high-end) with the hotel before travelling.
