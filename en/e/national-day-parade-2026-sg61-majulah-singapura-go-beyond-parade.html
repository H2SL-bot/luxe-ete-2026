National Day Parade 2026 — SG61 "Majulah Singapura, Go Beyond!" (national parade and fireworks, seen from the grand hotels)

9 August 2026 (previews on the preceding Saturdays; festivities at the Stadium Riverside Walk from 25 July to 9 August) · National Stadium (Kallang, 55 000 places); feux vers 19h30; salut presidentiel au canon sur le Kallang Basin… · Festivals & Music

Singapore's 61st National Day, themed "Majulah Singapura, Go Beyond!", and the first NDP at the National Stadium in 10 years. The grand hotels of Marina Bay (Fairmont, Mandarin Oriental, Ritz-Carlton Millenia, PARKROYAL Collection Marina Bay) offer staycations and dinners with fireworks views.

Line-up : Singapore's President and government; the Red Lions; RSAF aerial display

Access

Free parade tickets by Singpass ballot at ndp.gov.sg (residents only; applications closed on 6 June 2026). For visitors: hotel packages with a view (Fairmont, Mandarin Oriental, Ritz-Carlton Millenia, Marina Bay rooftops) or the previews on the preceding Saturdays.
