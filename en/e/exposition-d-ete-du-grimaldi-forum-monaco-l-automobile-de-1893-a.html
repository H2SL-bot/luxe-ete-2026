Grimaldi Forum summer exhibition: Monaco & the Automobile, from 1893 to the Present Day

1 July - 6 September 2026 · Grimaldi Forum, 10 avenue Princesse Grace, 98000 Monaco · Polo · Motoring · Sailing

The Grimaldi Forum's grand summer exhibition across 4,000 sq m: some fifty emblematic vehicles retracing more than 130 years of the automobile in the Principality, including over 20 cars victorious in the Monaco Grand Prix and the Monte-Carlo Rally (created in 1911), as well as models from the Princely Family's collection. From the elegance concours of the late 19th century to the Grand Prix created in 1929. Curated by Rodolphe Rapetti, chief curator…

Line-up : Collection of the Princely Family of Monaco; Automobile Club de Monaco; curator Rodolphe Rapetti

Access

Tickets online at grimaldiforum.com or on site: full price 15 EUR, free for under-18s. Access included in certain Monegasque palace-hotel packages (Société des Bains de Mer concierge).
