Copa del Rey MAPFRE 2026 — 44th edition (sailing)

From 1st to 8th August 2026 · Bay of Palma (regattas 4–8) · Baie de Palma — Real Club Náutico de Palma · Polo · Motoring · Sailing

The royal regatta of the Mediterranean: King Felipe VI races at the helm of the Aifos, surrounded by yacht owners and Spanish high society. Organised by the Real Club Náutico de Palma in the Bay of Palma, the 44th Copa del Rey MAPFRE combines prestige sailing with Mallorca's summer jet set.

Line-up : King Felipe VI (helmsman of the Aifos), international yacht owners, Spanish high society

How to get in

Real Club Náutico de Palma, organiser of the Copa del Rey MAPFRE (44th edition)

The regatta village at the Moll Vell is open free of charge to the public to get close to crews and boats. The truly high-society layer (owners' hospitality, evenings, royal entourage) is by private invitation: as no individual press contact is published in a verifiable way, contact the organisation via regatacopadelrey.com (Real Club Náutico de Palma, rcnp.es).
