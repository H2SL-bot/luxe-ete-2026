Sotheby's sale “Château Haut-Brion, Domaine Clarence Dillon” (90 years of the estate)

Thursday 1 October 2026 at 13:00 · Sotheby's Paris · Sotheby's Paris · Palace Hotels & Fine Dining

A historic ex-cellar sale: wines released directly from the cellars of Château Haut-Brion to mark 90 years of Dillon family ownership. Among the 676 lots, a barrel of the 2025 vintage — a first for a Premier Grand Cru Classé. The autumn rendezvous of Paris's great wine collectors.

Line-up : Major wine collectors, international trade, first-growth connoisseurs

How to get in

Sotheby's Paris (76 rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré) and Domaine Clarence Dillon, owner of Château Haut-Brion

Come to the public viewing, open to all: you can get close to the bottles and to the estate's team without having to bid.
