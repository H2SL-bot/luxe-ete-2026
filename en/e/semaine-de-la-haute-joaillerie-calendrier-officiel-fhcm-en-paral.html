High Jewelry Week (official FHCM calendar, running alongside couture)

3-9 July 2026 · Salons et hôtels particuliers des maisons (place Vendôme et environs) · Jewellery & Watchmaking

High jewelry presentations tied to couture week, on the official FHCM calendar: Chanel Joaillerie (3, 6, 7, 8/07), Boucheron (6-9/07), Messika (7-8/07), Hermès (7/07 morning), Anna Hu (7-8/07), Mikimoto (7/07), Dior Joaillerie (9/07 morning).

Line-up : Houses: Chanel, Boucheron, Messika, Hermès, Anna Hu, Mikimoto, Dior Joaillerie

How to get in

Fédération de la Haute Couture et de la Mode (FHCM), via the Chambre Syndicale de la Haute Couture (official calendar)

No ticketing or table: access only through the houses. Realistic route: become a high jewelry client of a participating house (ongoing purchases at the Place Vendôme boutique) — the client advisor registers their VICs for the private salon presentations, often at the Ritz. Otherwise, request a private appointment through the boutique, or go through a palace-hotel concierge. FHCM accreditation is reserved for media professionals. Prices: not published.
