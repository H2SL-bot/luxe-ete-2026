Stuart Weitzman "Exotic Escape" — pop-up at The Maidstone

2-31 July 2026 (early opening from 19 June according to some sources) · LDV at The Maidstone, 207 Main Street, East Hampton · Fashion & Couture

Stuart Weitzman's first Hamptons shopping installation: the "Exotic Escape" capsule (croc, leopard and zebra prints, wedges, platforms, statement sandals, men's shoes, eyewear), with a décor of stone floors, walnut shelving and watercolour seascape wallpaper inside the boutique hotel The Maidstone.

Access

Free entry during opening hours (11am-7pm); private appointments available through the brand.
