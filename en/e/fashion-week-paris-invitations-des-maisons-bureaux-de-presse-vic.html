Paris Fashion Week — house invitations: press offices & VIC

Week of 28 September to 6 October 2026 · shows by invitation · Sièges & boutiques des maisons (Paris) · Fashion & Couture

The royal road to the runway shows of the great houses: each house invites through its own press office (often a Parisian PR agency) and its VIC client service. ConstanceParis7 gathers the published professional contacts, verified one by one.

Line-up : Dior, Chanel, Saint Laurent, Louis Vuitton, Hermès, Loewe, Givenchy, Valentino, Balmain, Schiaparelli…

How to get in

Each house (Dior, Chanel, Saint Laurent, LV, Hermès, Loewe, Givenchy…) through its own press office or PR agency

Two paths. PRESS: obtain the free FHCM accreditation (accreditation@fhcm.paris), then write to the press office of the house you are targeting to request the show invitation — the contacts above are the published professional channels. VIC CLIENT (the route without being press): become a client of a house, stay loyal to one same sales advisor in the boutique and express the wish to be invited; a significant purchase history qualifies you for VIC status, which opens show invitations and the front row. No seat at the show is "bought": it is earned through the relationship. Free in both cases — it is the relationship (press or client) that grants access.
