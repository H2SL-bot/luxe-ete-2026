Jean Paul Gaultier Show — Duran Lantink's Couture Debut

8 July 2026, late afternoon · Paris (lieu non précisé par les sources; historiquement siège JPG, 325 rue Saint-Martin, Paris 3e) · Fashion & Couture

Duran Lantink's first haute couture collection as permanent creative director (the house's first CD after its founder): Louis XIV/Marie-Antoinette references crossed with futurism, digital scanning, 3D printing, industrial flocking and traditional tailoring. The most disruptive show of the week according to the press; Gaultier and Lantink embraced at the finale to an ovation.

Line-up : Duran Lantink; Jean Paul Gaultier, Teyana Taylor, Cardi B, Catherine Deneuve in the front row

How to get in

Jean Paul Gaultier (Puig group), a haute couture FW 2026-27 show listed on the official calendar of the Fédération de la Haute Couture et de la Mode (FHCM)

No ticketing or charity tables: access 100% by invitation (press, buyers, celebrities, couture clients). The most realistic path for a wealthy individual: become a client of the JPG Couture Salon (by appointment only, via Fashion@jpgaultier.fr / +33 1 72 75 83 00) — haute couture clients are invited to the shows. Otherwise, press accreditation via Lucien Pagès (contact@lucienpages.com). A palace concierge can approach the agency, without guarantee. Public: replay on JPG's channels and the FHCM site.
