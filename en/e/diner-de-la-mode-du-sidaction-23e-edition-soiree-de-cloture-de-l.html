Sidaction Dîner de la Mode (23rd edition) — couture closing gala

9 July 2026 (evening) · Lycée Carnot, 145 boulevard Malesherbes, 75017 Paris · Fashion & Couture

The grand charity dinner closing couture week, co-organised by the FHCM and Sidaction (the fight against HIV/AIDS), with Jean Paul Gaultier as its historic ambassador. The 2026 edition was marked by the broadcast on a giant screen of the World Cup quarter-final France-Morocco (10pm) during the dinner. The previous edition had raised EUR 705,000.

Line-up : Jean Paul Gaultier (ambassador), Simon Porte Jacquemus, Tina Kunakey, Farida Khelfa, Jean-Charles de Castelbajac, Béatrice Dalle, Bilal Hassani, Alizée, Zahia Dehar

How to get in

Sidaction and the Fédération de la Haute Couture et de la Mode (co-organised), an event founded by Pierre Bergé in 2003

No online ticketing published for 2026: tables and seats sold directly by Sidaction. Realistic route: write to Sidaction's communications/events department (the contact-us form or press contact n.naudi@sidaction.org) to buy a charity table — published benchmark from the 2020 edition: 15,000 € for a table of 10, 18,000 € for a table of 12; 2026 pricing not published. Alternative: invitation through an FHCM member house as a VIC client, or corporate sponsorship.
