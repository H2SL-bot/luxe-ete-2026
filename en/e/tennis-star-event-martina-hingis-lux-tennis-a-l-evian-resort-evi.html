Tennis Star Event — Martina Hingis (LUX Tennis) at the Evian Resort

3-4 August 2026 · Tennis & Rackets Sports Club de l'Evian Resort · Other

Highly exclusive LUX Tennis-format event with the legend Martina Hingis (former world No. 1, 5 Grand Slam titles): welcome cocktail and meet-and-greet on 3 August (6pm-7pm), adult and children's clinics on 4 August, exhibition matches, private lessons and one-to-one coaching. Category: tennis / prestige sport.

Line-up : Martina Hingis

Access

Booking through the Evian Resort (guests of the Royal/Ermitage hotels take priority) — via the official agenda or the concierge; private lessons at extra cost, prices on request; detailed experiences at luxtennis.com.
