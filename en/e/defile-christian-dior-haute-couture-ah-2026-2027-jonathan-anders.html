Christian Dior Haute Couture Show FW 2026-2027 (Jonathan Anderson)

6 July 2026, 2:30 PM · Pavillon en bois dans les jardins du musée Rodin, 77 rue de Varenne, Paris 7e · Fashion & Couture

Jonathan Anderson's second haute couture collection for Dior, translating the sculptural practice of American artist Lynda Benglis (silver and copper lamé, micro-pleated organza, 18th-century Indian chintz), in dialogue with works by Benglis displayed on the set.

Line-up : Jonathan Anderson (artistic director), works by Lynda Benglis

How to get in

Christian Dior Couture (LVMH group) — Jonathan Anderson show presented at the Musée Rodin, Paris 7th

Dior sells no tickets (official FAQ dior.com): guests are chosen by the Paris press department. Realistic route: become a VIC/haute couture client through an advisor at 30 avenue Montaigne (strong purchase history), the invitation coming via client relations; otherwise press accreditation for journalists. Failing that: the 'Grammar of Forms' scenography open to the public at the Musée Rodin from 7 to 12 July (museum ticket) and livestream on dior.com.
