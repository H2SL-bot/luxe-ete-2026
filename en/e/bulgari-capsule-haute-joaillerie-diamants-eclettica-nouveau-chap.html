Bulgari — “Eclettica” diamond high jewellery capsule (new chapter)

couture week, unveiled around 6 July 2026 · Paris (présentation privée pendant la semaine couture; lieu communiqué aux invités) · Jewellery & Watchmaking

New Parisian chapter of the Eclettica collection (unveiled in March 2026 in Milan): a capsule of 26 masterpieces entirely in diamonds, where the diamond is woven, draped and shaped like lace of light laid upon the body, with transformable pieces (a necklace whose pendant of five diamonds of more than 8 ct becomes a bracelet). Unveiling during couture week in July 2026 confirmed by several international media.

Line-up : Laura Burdese (CEO of Bulgari)

How to get in

Bulgari (LVMH group) — private presentation organised by the house during couture week, Paris, ~6 July 2026

Access 100% by house invitation, no ticketing or table (non-charity event). Realistic route: become a high jewellery client — an appointment at the 25 place Vendôme boutique (+33 1 55 35 00 50) or via bulgari.com, then build a purchase history followed by a client advisor to enter the VIC file invited to couture presentations. A palace concierge secures the HJ appointment, not the invitation. Press: Bulgari France press office.
