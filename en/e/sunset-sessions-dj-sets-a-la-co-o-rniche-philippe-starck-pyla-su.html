Sunset sessions & DJ sets at La Co(o)rniche (Philippe Starck)

All summer 2026 (July–August), ongoing · Hôtel 5* La Co(o)rniche, 46 avenue Louis Gaume, face au Banc d'Arguin et à la Dune du Pilat · Palace Hotels & Fine Dining

The most sought-after address on the Atlantic coast: a panoramic Starck-designed terrace lit by lanterns at sunset, a DJ at the outdoor bar until 2am in season, non-stop tapas service and views over the Banc d'Arguin. A jet-set crowd of celebrities and wealthy Bordeaux and Paris families. A standing offering of the hotel rather than a dated programme: parties are announced as the summer unfolds.

Access

Restaurant/bar booking all but essential in summer via lacoorniche.com or by telephone (+33 5 56 22 72 11); bar-terrace access subject to capacity; private hire available
