The Watermill Center Summer Benefit 2026 — « Limitless Time »

24-25 July 2026 (Friday and Saturday) · The Watermill Center, 39 Water Mill Towd Road, Water Mill, NY · Art & Auctions

The most exclusive — and most eccentric — art gala of the Hamptons summer, founded by Robert Wilson. 2026 edition confirmed: theme « Limitless Time », presented by Van Cleef & Arpels, the first edition since the passing of Robert Wilson in 2025. Closing of the International Summer Program (5 July-2 August 2026).

Line-up : New York art world, collectors, celebrities; 2026 lead partner: Van Cleef & Arpels

How to get in

The Watermill Center / The Byrd Hoffman Water Mill Foundation, an institution founded by Robert Wilson; 2026 edition 'Limitless Time' presented by Van Cleef & Arpels

Entirely paid gala, tickets and tables online via watermillcenter.org (Give Lively platform). Prices from recent editions: ~600 $ for the festival ticket, ~500 $ for those under 40, gala tables and sponsor packages well above that. Purchase also by email/phone (festival@watermillcenter.org, +1 631-726-4628). For a table, patronage or co-chairing: festival@watermillcenter.org. 'Byrds' members: reduced rates via byrds@watermillcenter.org. Tip: Saturday's Summer Benefit offers the broadest access; Friday's Artists Dinner is more exclusive.
