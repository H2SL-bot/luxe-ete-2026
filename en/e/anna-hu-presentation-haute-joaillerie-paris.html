Anna Hu — High Jewellery Presentation

7-8 July 2026 · Paris, lieu communiqué aux invités · Jewellery & Watchmaking

Presentation of the creations of the Chinese-American art jeweller Anna Hu (Anna Hu Haute Joaillerie), listed in the official FHCM couture week calendar. The maison's signature themes: nature and Chinese culture (orchids, butterflies), hand-painted nano-electroplated titanium, in partnership with Parisian high jewellery workshops.

Line-up : Anna Hu

How to get in

Anna Hu Haute Joaillerie (the maison itself), presentation listed in the official FHCM High Jewellery calendar

No charity table: a commercial presentation by appointment (7-8 July, 10am-6pm). Direct route: the annahu.com form (subject 'Make an appointment' or 'How to register'), application for the 'VIP membership' offered on the site, or an email to info@anna-hu.com introducing yourself as a potential buyer (one-of-a-kind pieces). The maison has a permanent showcase at the Ritz Paris and exhibits there during couture week: the Ritz concierge can support the request.
