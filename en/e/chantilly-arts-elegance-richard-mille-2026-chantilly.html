Chantilly Arts & Élégance Richard Mille 2026

Sunday 13 September 2026 · Château de Chantilly · Domaine du Château de Chantilly (jardins de Le Nôtre) · Palace Hotels & Fine Dining

The French-style concours d'élégance by Richard Mille: on Le Nôtre's parterre at the Château de Chantilly, exceptional cars and a judged couture show, art de vivre and a collector clientele. The ultra-VIP tier is experienced at the Concours and under the Garden Party marquees.

Line-up : Collectors, couture and jewellery houses, sponsor Richard Mille

How to get in

Peter Auto / Château de Chantilly, organisers of the Chantilly Arts & Élégance Richard Mille

A high-attendance event: to experience the truly social tier, aim for a VIP Garden Party package (marquees, champagne, Concours access) rather than general admission, via the official ticketing (chantillyartsetelegance.com/en/ticketing/). No individual press email is published — go through the official portal.
