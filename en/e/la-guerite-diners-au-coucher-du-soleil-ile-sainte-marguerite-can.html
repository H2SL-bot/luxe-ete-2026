La Guérite – sunset dinners, Île Sainte-Marguerite

Dinners Thursday, Friday & Saturday, from 25 June to October 2026 (from 7 pm) — recurring seasonal service, accessible only by boat · La Guérite, Île Sainte-Marguerite (accès par bateau) · Festivals & Music

Set on a rocky cove of Île Sainte-Marguerite, reachable only by boat, La Guérite transforms at dusk into a dinner facing the lights of Cannes. Mediterranean cuisine by Yiannis Kioroglou, yachts at anchor, resident DJs and a jet-set mood. Open booking via SevenRooms (boat shuttle departing from Port Canto).

Line-up : Resident DJs in the evening (occasional line-ups not announced in advance; no named headliner)

How to get in

Restaurant La Guérite

Dinner table booking via SevenRooms (sevenrooms.com/reservations/lagueritecannes) or directly with the restaurant; boat shuttle departing from Port Canto
