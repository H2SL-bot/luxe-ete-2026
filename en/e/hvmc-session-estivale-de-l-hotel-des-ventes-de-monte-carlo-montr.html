HVMC - Summer session of the Hôtel des Ventes de Monte-Carlo (Collector's Watches, Important Jewels, Yanou Collart Collection, Leather Good…

3-9 July 2026 (exhibition from 3 July, sales from 5 to 9 July) · Hôtel des Ventes de Monte-Carlo, 10-12 quai Antoine 1er, 98000 Monaco (sur le port) · Art & Auctions

Summer marathon by the HVMC house: five sale sessions in a single week on the port of Monaco - collector's watches, important jewels in two parts (signed pieces including Bulgari, sapphires, rubies, diamonds), luxury leather goods (Hermès, Dior) and the estate of Yanou Collart, agent to the stars (classic and signed jewels, pocket watches and bracelets including an engraved Tank Tiffany & Co given by Jerry Lewis, haute couture, bags).

Line-up : Estate of Yanou Collart (celebrity agent, close to Jerry Lewis); pieces by Hermès, Bulgari, Cartier, Tiffany & Co

Access

Free public exhibition from 3 to 9 July (10am-6pm). Bidding by registration with HVMC: in the room, by telephone or online (live) via hvmc.com, Drouot Digital, Invaluable or Interencheres. Contacts: jewelry department +377 93 25 54 94, watch department +377 93 25 54 82.
