Hotel Cala di Volpe Gala Night 2026 with Katy Perry

12 August 2026 · Hotel Cala di Volpe, a Luxury Collection Hotel (Marriott / Smeralda Holding) · Palace Hotels & Fine Dining

THE society event of the Italian summer: gala dinner, immersive staging and a private concert by Katy Perry (after Jennifer Lopez in 2025 and Mariah Carey in 2024). Confirmed by ANSA, L'Unione Sarda and the Consorzio Costa Smeralda (announcements of 11 May 2026). Very limited places, booking essential.

Line-up : Katy Perry (headliner); regulars: Gulf royals, billionaires, models and international celebrities moored on yachts offshore

How to get in

Hotel Cala di Volpe, a Luxury Collection Hotel (Marriott / Smeralda Holding), Porto Cervo — 'Something Special' / Gala Night event

Entirely paid evening, very limited capacity, by advance reservation with prepayment. Indicative prices (recent editions): seats of several thousand euros per person depending on the category (front row / private), dinner and show included, drinks not included. Book by email (reservations@somethingspecial.live) or via the event's official page. Tip: going through the concierge of the Cala di Volpe or of a luxury hotel on the Costa Smeralda significantly increases the chances of getting a seat.
