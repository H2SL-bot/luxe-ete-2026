L'Herbier Secret — The Chef's Table in the aromatic garden of the Hôtel de Crillon

From 12 June to 25 July, then from 27 August to 25 September 2026, Thursday to Saturday evenings. WARNING: CLOSED from 26 Jul… · Hôtel de Crillon, A Rosewood Hotel, 10 place de la Concorde, 75008 Paris — terrasse confidentielle du 2e étag… · Palace Hotels & Fine Dining

An ultra-confidential experience: a single table for 4 to 6 guests per evening in the palace's aromatic herb garden, a 5-course menu by executive chef Alan Taudon (plant- and sea-driven cuisine built around herbs grown on site), 250 EUR per guest, wine or alcohol-free pairings available as an option (+140 EUR).

Line-up : Alan Taudon (executive chef, formerly of the George V)

Access

Book directly with the Crillon's restaurant/concierge (rosewoodhotels.com/crillon) well in advance — only one table per service. For an August stay, aim exclusively for 27-29 August.
