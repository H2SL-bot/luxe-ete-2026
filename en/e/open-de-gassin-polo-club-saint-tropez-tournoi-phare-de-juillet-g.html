Open de Gassin — Polo Club Saint-Tropez (July's flagship tournament)

30 June - 26 July 2026 · Polo Club Saint-Tropez - Haras de Gassin, 1999 route du Bourrian, 83580 Gassin · Polo · Motoring · Sailing

The club's signature tournament, played in two categories (4-6 goals and 10-12 goals) with international teams. THE great July polo gathering on the peninsula (more than 50 teams, 110 players, 600 horses across the season). The 2026 dates are confirmed by the official Côte d'Azur France listing; the club's 2026 calendar is consistent (Sun Trophy 18-28 June beforehand, Polo Exhibition Week 27/07-2/08 afterwards).

Line-up : International teams and patrons from the European polo circuit; the gulf's jet-set crowd

Access

Free public access around the fields. VIP experience: reserve a table at the Restaurant du Polo 'Le 1999' or for the Sunday Polo brunch on 04 94 55 22 12 / contact@polo-st-tropez.com. Match schedule at www.polo-st-tropez.com and on Instagram @polosttropez.
