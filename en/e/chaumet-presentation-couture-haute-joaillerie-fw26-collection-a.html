Chaumet — FW26 couture high jewellery presentation ('A Journey Through Nature' collection)

6 July 2026 (reported by a single source, to be confirmed) · Paris, lieu non documenté (adresse historique : 12 place Vendôme) · Jewellery & Watchmaking

One source (5ELEVEN Magazine, published around 7 July 2026) reports a 'Chaumet Couture High Jewellery Fall Winter 2026' presentation on 6 July 2026 in Paris, following on from the 2026 collection 'A Journey Through Nature' (46 pieces, three botanical chapters) launched on 22 April 2026 with a gala at the Abbaye des Vaux-de-Cernay (Sophie Marceau, Song Hye-kyo, Natalia Vodianova). Chaumet does not appear on the FHCM High Jewellery calendar…

Line-up : Charles Leung (CEO); April gala: Sophie Marceau, Song Hye-kyo, Li Bingbing, Natalia Vodianova

How to get in

Maison Chaumet (LVMH group) — private in-house presentation, absent from the official FHCM High Jewellery calendar

No ticketing or table: private press/client presentation, outside the FHCM calendar. Realistic route: become a client — high jewellery appointment at 12 place Vendôme (appointment.chaumet.com, vendome@chaumet.com, +33 1 44 77 24 24) to see 'A Journey Through Nature'. Significant purchases lead to VIC status and invitations (presentations, galas such as at Vaux-de-Cernay). A palace concierge can also arrange a private viewing.
