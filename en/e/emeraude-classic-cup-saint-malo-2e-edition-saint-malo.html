Émeraude Classic Cup – Saint-Malo (2nd edition)

10 – 15 August 2026 (welcome on the 10th, racing from 11 to 15 August) · Baie de Saint-Malo (organisation Société Nautique de la Baie de Saint-Malo) · Polo · Motoring · Sailing

Five days of exceptional classic-yacht racing, strictly by invitation: Mariquita, Moonbeam III and IV, P Class and grand traditional yachts announced. Welcome and briefing on 10 August, racing in the bay from 11 to 15, crews' dinner on the 13th, prize-giving and cocktail reception at the yacht club on the evening of the 15th. The pinnacle of prestige sailing in the Saint-Malo summer.

Line-up : Owners and crews of the international classic yachting circuit (Fife/Herreshoff-type yachts)
