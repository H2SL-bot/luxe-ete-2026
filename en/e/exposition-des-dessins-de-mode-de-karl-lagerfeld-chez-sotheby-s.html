Exhibition of Karl Lagerfeld's Fashion Drawings at Sotheby's Paris (ahead of the auction)

2-7 July 2026 (online sale 1-8 July) · Sotheby's Paris, 83 rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré, Paris 8e · Art & Auctions

Over 1,000 previously unseen sketches by Karl Lagerfeld (from the 1960s to 2019, work for Chanel, Fendi and Chloé) shown free of charge before their dispersal in the online auction 'KARL, Karl Lagerfeld's Estate VI, Inspirations' — all lots without reserve, bidding starting from one euro. Timed to coincide with couture week.

Line-up : Works by Karl Lagerfeld (Chanel, Fendi, Chloé)

Access

Free exhibition open to all at Sotheby's; to bid, register at sothebys.com (online bidding from 1 euro, no reserve).
