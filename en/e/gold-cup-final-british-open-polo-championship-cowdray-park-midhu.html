Gold Cup Final — British Open Polo Championship (Cowdray Park)

Final on 19 July 2026 (tournament from 24 June to 19 July) · Cowdray Park Polo Club, The Lawns · Polo · Motoring · Sailing

The final of Europe's most prestigious polo tournament (22-goal High Goal level), 'the home of British polo'. A highly social day: luxury hospitality, champagne, the divot-stomping tradition, grandstands and private boxes. NB: the Cartier Queen's Cup at Guards Polo Club is played in mid-June (outside the July–August window).

Access

Tickets and hospitality via cowdraypolo.co.uk; VIP packages (Grandstand, Seriously Cool Events)
