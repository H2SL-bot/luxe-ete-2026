La Galerie Dior — museum of the House of Dior, 30 avenue Montaigne

all of July 2026 (open daily except Tuesday; new summer 2026 route) · La Galerie Dior, 11 rue François 1er / 30 av. Montaigne, Paris 8e · Art & Auctions

A permanent route + temporary exhibitions inside Christian Dior's historic town house: haute couture gowns, ateliers, archives; the scenography is refreshed for summer 2026 with the creations of the new artistic director Jonathan Anderson. The most direct public doorway into a great couture house. Ongoing.

Access

Booking a time slot is strongly recommended at billetterie.galeriedior.com (only tickets purchased on the official site guarantee entry, full price 16 EUR); a few tickets are sold on site.
