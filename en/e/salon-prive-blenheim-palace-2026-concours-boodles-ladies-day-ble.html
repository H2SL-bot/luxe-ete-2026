Salon Privé Blenheim Palace 2026 — Concours & Boodles Ladies' Day

From 2 to 6 September 2026 · Blenheim Palace (Concours 2-3, Boodles Ladies' Day 4) · Blenheim Palace, Woodstock, Oxfordshire · Palace Hotels & Fine Dining

The aristocratic garden party of the British collector-car calendar: on the lawns of the ducal Blenheim Palace, Salon Privé brings together a collectors' concours d'élégance, champagne and a fashion-and-jewellery day orchestrated by the house of Boodles. One of England's most exclusive gatherings.

Line-up : Car collectors, British aristocracy, the house of Boodles (jewellery)

How to get in

Salon Privé, organiser of the Concours (by Aviva Private Clients) and of the Boodles Ladies' Day at Blenheim Palace

Access is through the official ticketing (salonpriveconcours.com/buy-tickets/), from 60 £ for general admission to 645 £ for luxury hospitality. To genuinely mingle with the concours world, aim for the Concours days (Wednesday-Thursday, hospitality) and the Boodles Ladies' Day (Friday). As no individual press email is published, go through the official portal.
