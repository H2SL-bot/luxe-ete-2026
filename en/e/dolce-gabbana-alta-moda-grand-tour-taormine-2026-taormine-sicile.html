Dolce & Gabbana Alta Moda Grand Tour — Taormina 2026

12-16 July 2026 · San Domenico Palace (Four Seasons), Radicepura (Giarre), Teatro Antico di Taormina, La Plage Resort (Isola Be… · Fashion & Couture

Dolce & Gabbana's Alta Moda returns to Taormina 14 years after the inaugural 2012 edition: five days of Alta Gioielleria/Alta Moda/Alta Sartoria presentations and galas between the San Domenico Palace, the Teatro Antico and Isola Bella. THE gathering of the post-couture jet-set circuit in the Mediterranean, confirmed by the Sicilian press and Italian event guides.

Line-up : Around 500 international clients and celebrities expected; previous Alta Moda editions have brought together Madonna, Monica Bellucci, Carla Bruni, Eva Herzigova, Catherine Deneuve (the presence of Michele Morrone, announced on the initial list, could not be confirmed by an independent source)

How to get in

Dolce & Gabbana S.r.l.; production by Balich Wonder Studio (Milan), with the Municipality of Taormina, Parco Naxos and Fondazione Taormina

No ticketing. Route 1: become an Alta Moda client — make yourself known to a D&G boutique as a couture buyer; the founders personally validate the list (~500 clients). Route 2: charity lot — the Naples Winter Wine Festival sold on 31/01/2026 the 'Grand Tour of D&G Alta Moda' lot (2 people, 12-17 July 2026, festival entry from $17,500/couple, hammer price not published). Press: accreditation via press@dolcegabbana.it.
