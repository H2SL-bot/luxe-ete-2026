New Year's Eve at Bagatelle Gustavia

31 December 2026 (annual fixture, 2026-27 menu forthcoming) · Gustavia, Saint-Barthélemy · Polo · Motoring · Sailing

Gustavia's festive institution facing the superyachts: a dinner-show that turns into a party after midnight, with an unobstructed view of the harbour fireworks. Every 31 December, Bagatelle (a brand born in Saint-Tropez/New York) hosts one of the island's most sought-after tables; reservations close months in advance. The 2026-27 edition has not yet been published, but the tradition holds constant.

Line-up : Yachting crowd and Franco-American jet set; a 'Saint-Tropez of the Caribbean' atmosphere
