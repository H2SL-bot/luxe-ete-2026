Twiga Porto Cervo — Ozuna (Live Urbano)

26 July 2026 · Twiga Porto Cervo · Festivals & Music

VERIFIED (DJ Mag Italia + La Nuova Sardegna): headline night with reggaeton superstar Ozuna at Twiga, one of the urbano peaks of the Costa Smeralda season.

Line-up : Ozuna

How to get in

Twiga Porto Cervo (formerly Billionaire), a beach club-restaurant operated by LMDV Hospitality / LDVM Hospitality (part of the group of Leonardo Maria Del Vecchio, LMDV Capital, which bought the Twiga brand and the Billionaire of Porto Cervo from Flavio Briatore in 2025). Opening on June 12, 2026; the Ozuna evening is part of the Twiga Club's live line-up.

No general-public ticketing: access is by booking a table or a bed for the evening. Two official routes per event on the Twiga calendar: 'BOOK TABLE' (WhatsApp +39 348 722 7960) and 'BUY TICKETS' (book-now page twigaworld.com); table reservation also possible via SevenRooms. Otherwise go through your hotel concierge or a Costa Smeralda concierge service (Porto Cervo Reservation, +39 351 0192462). Very sought-after night (reggaeton headliner): book several weeks in advance, high minimum spend. No free entry: an upscale private club. NB: other numbers circulate on third-party booking sites (topreservation, etc.) but are not confirmed as Twiga's official line — favor the official routes above.
