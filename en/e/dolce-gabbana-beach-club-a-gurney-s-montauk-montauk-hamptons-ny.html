Dolce & Gabbana Beach Club at Gurney's Montauk

9 June - 26 September 2026 for the boutique; beach club until Labor Day (ongoing throughout July) · Gurney's Montauk Resort & Seawater Spa, 290 Old Montauk Highway · Fashion & Couture

The return of Dolce & Gabbana's seaside takeover at Gurney's (the house's first beach takeover in the United States), marking the hotel's centenary year: East Deck dressed in Blu Mediterraneo majolica prints (cabanas, parasols, loungers), a pop-up boutique, restaurant and cocktails in the colours of the Dolce Vita.

Line-up : Celebrities and models passing through Montauk

Access

Beach and cabana access via Gurney's reservation (hotel guests given priority, day passes subject to availability); the pop-up boutique is open to all; book through the Gurney's concierge.
