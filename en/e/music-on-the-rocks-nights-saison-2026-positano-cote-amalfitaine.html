Music on the Rocks — nights (2026 season)

Summer 2026 season — club open every night (approx. 17:00–04:00); house/dance DJ sets and guest artists per the calendar (line-up released throughout the season, not yet dated) · Music on the Rocks — club troglodyte creusé dans la falaise · Festivals & Music

A one-of-a-kind club carved into the rock at the water's edge, the benchmark of Amalfi clubbing for forty years. After dinner you descend for house and dance DJ sets until dawn, in a grotto-like setting facing the sea of Positano — chic dress code, private tables and an intimate atmosphere prized by the coast's celebrity crowd.
