Open de France de Polo — Polo Club du Domaine de Chantilly (club's 30th anniversary)

From 3 to 20 September 2026 · Polo Club du Domaine de Chantilly · Polo Club du Domaine de Chantilly, La Ferme d'Apremont · Polo · Motoring · Sailing

The great fixture of French polo, forty minutes from Paris, marking thirty years of the club founded by Patrick Guerrand-Hermès. Sixteen international teams and more than 1,400 horses on site, with the Open de France Féminin from 15 September. A discreet, thoroughly Parisian society crowd in the stands.

Line-up : International polo patrons, equestrian families, Parisian clientele

How to get in

Polo Club du Domaine de Chantilly, La Ferme d'Apremont (60300 Apremont)

Aim for the weekend finals: match commentary, prize-giving and cocktails, in a far more intimate atmosphere than Deauville.
