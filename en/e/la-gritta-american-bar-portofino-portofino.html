La Gritta American Bar — Portofino

Summer season 2026 (July–August) · Ponton flottant sur le port de Portofino (Calata Marconi 20 / Molo Umberto I) · Other

Portofino's historic American bar on its floating pontoon: THE sunset aperitivo spot for watching the chic set parade around the harbour. Portofino's nightlife is concentrated on the Piazzetta.

Line-up : Milanese regulars and the harbour's international clientele

Access

No invitation required, though booking is advised in high season. A historic bar (1954, refurbished in 2001) on a floating pontoon: sunset cocktails and people-watching over the harbour parade. The floating terrace can be privatised for events.
