Weaving, Embroidering, Sublimating. The Savoir-Faire of Fashion — Palais Galliera

13 December 2025 – 18 October 2026 (all of July) · Palais Galliera, 10 avenue Pierre-Ier-de-Serbie, 75116 Paris · Fashion & Couture

The first chapter of a trilogy devoted to the crafts of fashion: more than 350 works from the museum's collections centred on weaving, printing, embroidery, lace and artificial flowers, with the participation of le19M (Chanel).

Line-up : le19M (Chanel Métiers d'art)

Access

Tickets at palaisgalliera.paris.fr; online booking recommended.
