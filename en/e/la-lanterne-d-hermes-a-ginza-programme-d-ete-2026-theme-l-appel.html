The Hermès Lanterne in Ginza — summer 2026 programme (theme "The Call of the Open Sea", Flâneur Tour, window displays)

Summer 2026 (programming active in August, precise dates by activity) · Ginza Maison Hermes, 5-4-1 Ginza · Other

An immersive Hermès programme centred on the Ginza Maison around the house's 2026 annual theme "The Call of the Open Sea": narrative window displays (including the summer series "A piece of adventure in every window"), a "Flâneur Tour" featuring artists, architects and filmmakers close to the house, and audio experiences.

Line-up : Hermès; Fondation d'entreprise Hermès

Access

Windows and the tour are freely accessible; activities by registration via lanterne.hermes.com and the Hermès Japan booking system. Details of the August Skills Academy workshops are yet to be confirmed on the website in July.
