Elie Saab Haute Couture Show FW 2026-2027

8 July 2026, 2:30 pm · Lieu non précisé par les sources · Fashion & Couture

An Elie Saab couture show inspired by the mythology of the great masked balls (Truman Capote's Black and White Ball, Old Hollywood): corseted velvet gowns, brocades, New Look silhouettes, fluid tuxedos and an expanded menswear couture offering. Broadcast live.

Line-up : Elie Saab

How to get in

ELIE SAAB (ELIE SAAB group), a show listed on the official FHCM calendar of Paris Haute Couture Week

No charity tables: a house show, invitations controlled by the press office and the client-relations team. A realistic path: become a haute couture client — a private appointment at the Paris salon (43 av. Franklin D. Roosevelt, 75008) via hautecouture-fr@eliesaab.com, the website form or the couture line +33 6 34 45 57 11; active couture clients are invited to the shows. A palace concierge can help arrange this appointment. Otherwise, press accreditation through the press office, or the free public livestream…
