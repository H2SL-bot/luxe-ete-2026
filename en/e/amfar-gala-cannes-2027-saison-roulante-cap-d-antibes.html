amfAR Gala Cannes 2027 (rolling season)

May 2027 during the Cannes Film Festival — the 2026 edition (21 May) has already taken place; 2027 date estimated, to be confirmed by amfAR · Hôtel du Cap-Eden-Roc, Cap d'Antibes · Festivals & Music

The most glamorous charity gala on the Riviera, set beside the gardens of the Hôtel du Cap-Eden-Roc: a prestige dinner, a fashion show, a live auction and a concert by a superstar (Robbie Williams, Zara Larsson and PinkPantheress were on the 2026 line-up). A genuine, published route in via the purchase of a seat or a table from amfAR.

Line-up : The 2027 line-up has not yet been announced (2026 reference: Robbie Williams, Zara Larsson, PinkPantheress)

How to get in

amfAR (The Foundation for AIDS Research)

Purchase of a seat or a table via the official amfAR ticketing (events.amfar.org) or by contacting the amfAR Cannes office
