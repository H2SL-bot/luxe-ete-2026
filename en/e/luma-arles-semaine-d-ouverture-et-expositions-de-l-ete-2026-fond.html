LUMA Arles — Opening Week and Summer 2026 Exhibitions (Maja Hoffmann Foundation)

Opening week 4-7 July 2026; summer exhibitions from 4 July to 8 November 2026 and beyond · LUMA Arles, Parc des Ateliers (tour Frank Gehry, Grande Halle) · Art & Auctions

Maja Hoffmann's foundation opens its summer season with three major exhibitions: 'CORRESPONDENCES' by Soundwalk Collective & Patti Smith at the Grande Halle (4 July-8 November), 'Bodies Never Lie' by Stan Douglas and 'Amanat, The Sacred Forest' by Saodat Ismailova. LUMA also hosts the Dior Photography and Visual Arts Award for young talents, and the PRINT platform (food, music, image) on the east side of the campus.

Line-up : Maja Hoffmann, Patti Smith, Stephan Crasneanscki (Soundwalk Collective), Stan Douglas, Saodat Ismailova, Hans Ulrich Obrist; presence of the house of Dior via its young talents award

Access

Many free events without reservation (Patti Smith signing, talks, DJ sets, PRINT); exhibitions and screenings by ticket via tickets.luma.org; the Patti Smith performances of 5-6 July are already sold out. Daily access to the Parc des Ateliers all summer.
