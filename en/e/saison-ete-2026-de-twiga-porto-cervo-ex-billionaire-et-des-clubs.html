Summer 2026 season at Twiga Porto Cervo (formerly Billionaire) and the Costa Smeralda clubs

throughout August 2026 (peak from 1 to 20 August; LMDV venues open since June 2026) · Twiga Porto Cervo (ex-Billionaire), Casa Fiori Chiari, Vesta, Crazy Pizza, Zuma, Langosteria, Nammos Baja Sar… · Other

Confirmed: Billionaire no longer exists — Flavio Briatore has handed over Twiga and the legendary club to Leonardo Maria Del Vecchio (LMDV Hospitality), a transformation effective since summer 2025. For 2026, the LMDV cluster in Porto Cervo has reopened in three formats: Casa Fiori Chiari (11 June), Twiga (12 June) and the fine-dining Vesta (26 June), with Twiga Club and international DJs upstairs. The Nammos residency at Phi Beach (Baja Sardinia) is also confirmed.

Line-up : Leonardo Maria Del Vecchio (LMDV owner), international jet-set clientele, guest DJs and artists

Access

Table reservation essential in August (the Ferragosto weeks are all but impossible without a concierge): venue websites and Instagram, platforms such as Xceed for the club, or hotel concierge (Cala di Volpe, Pitrizza, Romazzino, Cervo); Nammos: nammos@phibeach.com / WhatsApp +39 376 2803469
