Ravello Festival – "Götterdämmerung" conducted by Kent Nagano

Friday 31 July 2026 · Belvedere di Villa Rufolo (scène suspendue sur la mer) · Festivals & Music

An exceptional Wagnerian evening of the 74th edition: Twilight of the Gods in concert performance by the Dresdner Festspielorchester and Concerto Köln under the baton of Kent Nagano, on the most spectacular belvedere in the Mediterranean. The most coveted date of the Amalfi Coast's musical summer, treasured by the music-loving elite in residence at the Caruso and Palazzo Avino.

Line-up : Kent Nagano, Dresdner Festspielorchester, Concerto Köln

How to get in

Fondazione Ravello, a musical and cultural body under the aegis of the Campania Region, organiser of the Ravello Festival (74th edition, 2026) on the Belvedere of Villa Rufolo.

Ticket at 60 EUR via the official box office (ravellofestival.com or the Piazza Duomo box office); limited seats on the Belvedere of Villa Rufolo, book early. Under 26 discount (20 EUR on site, excluding the Concerto all'alba). Free route: press accreditation via the press office (ufficiostampa@fondazioneravello.it) or Lucia Serino (l.serino@fondazioneravello.it). Music-loving audiences stay at the Caruso Belmond and the Palazzo Avino.
