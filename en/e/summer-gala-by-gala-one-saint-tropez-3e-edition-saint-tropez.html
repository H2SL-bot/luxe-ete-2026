Summer Gala by Gala One Saint-Tropez (3rd edition)

Thursday, July 23, 2026, Golf-Club de Saint-Tropez (venue disclosed to guests) · Domaine privé de la presqu'île de Saint-Tropez (lieu communiqué aux invités ; édition 2025 au golf de Saint-T… · Other

Ultra-exclusive charity gala, the society successor to the great Saint-Tropez galas: cocktail reception, seated dinner, auction and private concert, in aid of child protection, biodiversity and water (partner One Drop). The official site confirms a 'July 2026' edition in Saint-Tropez; the 2026 host and performers have not yet been announced. Past editions: Jamie Foxx (2025 host), Kylie Minogue, Rita Ora.

Line-up : Past editions: Jamie Foxx, Leonardo DiCaprio, Kylie Minogue, Rita Ora, Camila Cabello, Kate Hudson; 2026 line-up not announced

How to get in

Gala One (prod. iSociety Group) — founding chairs Leonardo Maria Del Vecchio and Sinan Tuna, chairman Tommaso Chiabra

Invitation-only at the Golf-Club Saint-Tropez (July 23, 2026, according to The Sybarite). Direct route: write to GalaOne@iSocietyGroup.com for a charity table or tickets (prices not published, on request). Concierge services (The Sybarite, Royist) offer seats, but the organizer warns against unauthorized resellers. Sponsorship or a donation to the causes (Amend, Rainforest Trust, WELL/BEINGS) lends credibility to the request. No telephone number published.
