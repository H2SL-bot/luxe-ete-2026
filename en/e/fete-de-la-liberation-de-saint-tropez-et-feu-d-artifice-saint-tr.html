Saint-Tropez Liberation celebrations and fireworks display

14-15 August 2026 (fireworks on 14 August) · Vieux port et baie de Saint-Tropez (feu tiré du Môle Jean-Réveille) · Other

Commemoration of the Provence landings (15 August 1944): the liveliest day of the summer, with the grand fireworks display fired over the gulf the evening before — yachts take up position in the bay from early evening.

Access

Free, open access on the port. To watch from the sea: yacht hire/day charter through the port's charter companies or a concierge; the rooftops and portside restaurants (Hôtel de Paris, Sube) are booked up weeks in advance.
