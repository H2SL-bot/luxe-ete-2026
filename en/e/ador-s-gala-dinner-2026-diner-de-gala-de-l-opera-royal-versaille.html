ADOR's Gala Dinner 2026 — Opéra Royal Gala Dinner

Saturday 3 October 2026, from 16:30 · Opéra Royal, Château de Versailles · Festivals & Music

ADOR's 9th charity gala dinner in aid of the Opéra Royal: champagne reception (Salle des Croisades), gala concert 'From Salzburg to Naples: lyric jewels of Mozart, Rossini, Donizetti', cocktails in the Salon d'Hercule, a musical interlude in the Chapelle Royale and a promenade through the Grands Appartements, grand supper in the Galerie des Batailles, then fireworks from the Galerie des Glaces. A patronage dinner of the highest order, with individual seats or tables of 10, open to donors (patronage tax deduction).

Line-up : Gwendoline Blondeel (soprano) and soloists of the Académie de l'Opéra Royal; Choir and Orchestra of the Opéra Royal under the baton of Gaétan Jarry (Mozart / Rossini / Donizetti programme)

How to get in

ADOR — Les Amis de l'Opéra Royal de Versailles

Bienfaiteur seat €1,400 or Grand Bienfaiteur €1,600; Grand Bienfaiteur package with a night at the Waldorf Astoria Trianon Palace Versailles €3,700 (for two); Prestige table €16,000 or Premium €20,000 (10 seats). Patronage tax deduction. Online booking closed: contact the ADOR office (official phone/email) or the box office on 01 30 83 78 89 (Mon–Fri 11:00–18:00), 3 bis rue des Réservoirs, 78000 Versailles.
