Jingu Gaien Fireworks Festival 2026 - 45th edition (fireworks with premium hotel packages)

8 August 2026 (postponed to 9 August in case of inclement weather) · Meiji Jingu Gaien (Jingu Stadium et Chichibunomiya Rugby Ground) · Festivals & Music

Tokyo's great urban fireworks display (45th edition), the only one in the city paired with live concerts, staged in the stadiums of Jingu Gaien. On the luxury side: dinner-with-a-view plans at the Hotel New Otani Tokyo and packages at the Aoyama Grand Hotel.

Line-up : Japanese artists in concert (line-up announced in summer)

Access

Paid tickets for stadium seats via jinguhanabi.com, eplus.jp and pia.jp (from around 7,000 yen in 2025); dinner-and-view packages at the Hotel New Otani (www.newotani.co.jp/tokyo/hanabi/), on sale from June-July.
