Saint-Tropez Fine Art 2026 (David Pluskwa pop-up museum)

8 July - 15 August 2026 · Salle Jean-Despas (rez-de-chaussée), 16 boulevard Vasserot, place des Lices · Art & Auctions

Gallerist David Pluskwa's 16th summer season, in partnership with the Galerie Ary Jan: a free pop-up museum of Impressionist, modern, post-war and contemporary art (Renoir, Niki de Saint Phalle's 'Ange luminaire', François-Xavier Lalanne, silversmith Goudji). It succeeds the PAD in the same Salle Jean-Despas.

Line-up : Works by Renoir, Niki de Saint Phalle, François-Xavier Lalanne, Goudji

Access

Free admission during opening hours; information at the Saint-Tropez Tourist Office (sainttropeztourisme.com).
