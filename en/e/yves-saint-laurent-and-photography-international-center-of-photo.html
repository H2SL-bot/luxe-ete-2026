Yves Saint Laurent and Photography — International Center of Photography (during the closure of the Musée YSL Paris)

11 June - 28 September 2026 (all of August) · International Center of Photography (ICP), 84 Ludlow Street (angle Essex Street, Lower East Side), New York,… · Fashion & Couture

Nearly 300 photographs and archival objects from the Musée Yves Saint Laurent Paris and the Fondation Pierre Bergé-YSL (Avedon, Beaton, Bourdin, Horst, Klein, Leibovitz, Meisel, Newton, Penn, Seidner, Warhol...), on view in New York during the renovation closure of the Paris museum. Worth noting for a stop in NYC in August.

Line-up : Musée Yves Saint Laurent Paris / Fondation Pierre Bergé - YSL

Access

Tickets at icp.org.
