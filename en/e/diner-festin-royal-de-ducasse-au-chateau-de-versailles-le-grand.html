Ducasse's 'Festin Royal' dinner at the Château de Versailles — Le Grand Contrôle (Airelles)

throughout the month of July 2026 (daily dinners) · Airelles Château de Versailles — Le Grand Contrôle, dans l'enceinte du château (bâtiment de 1681 signé Jules… · Palace Hotels & Fine Dining

Alain Ducasse's restaurant in the former Grand Contrôle building, at the heart of the Versailles estate: the 'Festin Royal' reinterprets royal recipes with period-style service (the 'Le Grand Festin' menu at around EUR 300). An ideal pairing with Saturday's Grandes Eaux Nocturnes or the 14 July evening. The 'Grand Banquet' (dinner-show) takes place only a few times a year, with July dates not documented.

Line-up : Alain Ducasse

Access

Reservation on airelles.com or at +33 1 85 36 05 50, at least 24h in advance; the 'Le Dîner, Le Festin Royal' gift box available on gifts.airelles.com. Privileged access to the château and gardens for hotel guests.
