Vogue World: Milano 2026

Tuesday 22 September 2026 · Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, Milan · Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, Milan · Fashion & Couture

Anna Wintour transforms the Galleria into a monumental stage: the most sought-after fashion evening of the year. A travelling show by Vogue, its first Italian edition at the heart of Milan's drawing room.

Line-up : Anna Wintour, stars and models, Italian houses

How to get in

Vogue / Conde Nast (a travelling event created by Anna Wintour) and Vogue Italia, in partnership with the City of Milan (Comune di Milano / YesMilano)

An evening show at the Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II on 22 September 2026 (opening day of Milano Fashion Week), livestreamed worldwide. As with previous editions (New York, London, Paris, Los Angeles), access is via Vogue ticketing — proceeds donated to a cause (here, the revitalisation of a Milan neighbourhood with the City) — and via Vogue/house invitations for the front row. No named press contact has been published to date: follow the official YesMilano page and Vogue Italia's channels for the 2026 ticketing arrangements. Professional avenue: accreditation and press relations through the Vogue Italia press office (Conde Nast Italia).
