Paris Haute Couture Week Autumn-Winter 2026-2027 (official FHCM calendar)

6-9 July 2026 · Multiples venues: Petit Palais, Grand Palais, Musée Rodin, Cité Internationale Universitaire, salons des mais… · Fashion & Couture

Official haute couture week organised by the Fédération de la Haute Couture et de la Mode (FHCM), ~30 houses presenting the Autumn-Winter 2026-2027 collections. An edition marked by the couture debuts of Pierpaolo Piccioli at Balenciaga and Duran Lantink at Jean Paul Gaultier, and the first appearances of Manish Malhotra and Standing Ground.

Line-up : Bad Bunny, Michelle Yeoh, Emma Corrin, Tilda Swinton, Catherine Deneuve, Cardi B, Demi Moore; Jonathan Anderson (Dior), Matthieu Blazy (Chanel), Pierpaolo Piccioli (Balenciaga), Duran Lantink (Jean Paul Gaultier), Manish Malhotra (Bollywood debut)

How to get in

Fédération de la Haute Couture et de la Mode (FHCM), 100 rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré, 75008 Paris

No ticketing: seats are not for sale. The royal route: become a haute couture client — appointments at the couture salons (Dior on av. Montaigne, Chanel on rue Cambon) via private client relations; couture buyers are invited to their house's shows. Alternatives: specialist concierges (Sincura, VIP Concierge, ~5,000-7,000 GBP/show, no guarantee for the major houses), FHCM press accreditation for professionals, free FHCM livestreams.
