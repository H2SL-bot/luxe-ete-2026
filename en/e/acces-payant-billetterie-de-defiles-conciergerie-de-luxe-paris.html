How to get in

Independent ticketing platforms & luxury concierges (outside the official FHCM calendar)

A fully paid route, no press or client status required. AFFORDABLE: Fashion Week Online sells seats to independent designers' shows (~€150 per show, up to VIP tiers). HIGH-END: concierges AC Private (3-to-8-day packages from €8,000 to €20,000) and Sincura (on quote) craft bespoke access and itineraries. Important: these shows are independent / 'off-calendar', distinct from the official shows of the major houses (which are not sold).
