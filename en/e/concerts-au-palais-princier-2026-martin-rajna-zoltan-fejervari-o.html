Concerts at the Prince's Palace 2026 — Martin Rajna & Zoltán Fejérvári (OPMC)

2 August 2026, 9.30pm · Cour d'Honneur du Palais Princier de Monaco · Art & Auctions

A concert by the Monte-Carlo Philharmonic Orchestra in the Court of Honour of the Prince's Palace, a tradition created by Rainier III and Princess Grace more than 60 years ago. Conducted by Martin Rajna (principal conductor of the Hungarian State Opera), with pianist Zoltán Fejérvári: Beethoven's Piano Concerto No. 3 in C minor, Op. 37 and Dvořák's Symphony No. 8 in G major, Op. 88.

Line-up : Martin Rajna (conductor), Zoltán Fejérvári (piano); concerts given at the invitation of H.S.H. Prince Albert II in his residence

Access

Online ticketing at opmc.mc or montecarloticket.com (until 2h before the concert); OPMC box office at the Atrium of the Casino de Monte-Carlo, Place du Casino, tel. +377 92 00 13 70. 2026 prices: Carré d'Or 175 €, then from 120 € to 20 € (Cat. 5); group (10+) and youth (under 25) rates. Formal city attire required, jacket and tie compulsory for gentlemen. Tickets still available as of verification (July 2026).
